- Damage Equation
	- $d_{total} = d_{raw} * (1 - m_{SR} + m_{SRR}) * (1 + m_{SA})$
		- $m_{SR} = 1-\Pi (1 - {R_S}_i)$
			- $R_S$: spirit resist
		- $m_{SRR} = 1 - \Pi (1 - {R_{SR}}_i)$
			- $R_{SR}$: spirit resist reduction
		- $m_{SA} = \Pi (1 + {SA}_i) - 1$
			- ${SA}_i$: spirit amp
