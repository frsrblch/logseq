- Rotation as two reflections
	-
	- $\bold a = a_x \hat{\bold x} + a_y \hat{\bold y} + a_z \hat{\bold z}$
	- $\bold {ab} = \bold a \cdot \bold b + \bold a \wedge \bold b$
		- $\bold a \cdot \bold b = \frac 1 2 (\bold {ab} + \bold {ba})$
		- $\bold a \wedge \bold b = \frac 1 2 (\bold {ab} - \bold {ba})$
	- $P_{\bold a}(\bold b) = (\bold b \cdot \bold a) \bold a^{-1}$
	- $R_{\bold a}(\bold b) = (\bold b \wedge \bold a) \bold a^{-1}$
	- $\text{Ref}_{\bold a}(\bold b) = P_{\bold a}(\bold b) - R_{\bold a}(\bold b)$
	                  $= (\bold b \cdot \bold a - \bold b \wedge \bold a) \bold a^{-1}$
	                  $= (\bold a \cdot \bold b + \bold a \wedge \bold b) \bold a^{-1}$
	                  $= \bold a \bold b \bold a^{-1}$
	- $\text{Rot}_{\bold a \rightarrow \bold c}(\bold b) = \bold {caba}^{-1} \bold c^{-1}$
	- $\bold b$ is rotated by twice the angle between $\bold a$ and $\bold c$
		- $\bold R = \bold {ca}$
		- $\bold R^{-1} = (\bold {ca})^{-1} = \bold a^{-1} \bold c^{-1}$
		- $\displaystyle\sqrt R = \frac{R + 1}{|R + 1|}$
	- $\text{Rot}_{\bold a \rightarrow \bold c}(\bold b) = \bold {RbR}^{-1}$
